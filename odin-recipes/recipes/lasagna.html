Lasagna

Easy to make delicious Lasagna

This classic lasagna is made with easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with cheese then bake until it's bubbly! Great for feeding a big family or keeping in freezer until later.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28-ounce tomato sauce
  • 3 ounce tomato paste
  • 14 ounce crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon red/white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodle
  • 15 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 24 ounces mozzarella cheese grated/slice
  • 4 ounches freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Cool and serve

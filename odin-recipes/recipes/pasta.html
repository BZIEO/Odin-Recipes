Penne Alla Vodka

Creamy and rich delicious pasta meal

This creamy and flavorful pasta just can't be beat! It's super easy to make and in 30 minutes you'll have an amazing dinner on the table!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 28 ounce can italian style whole tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps to make

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water
  2. Prepare sauce while pasta is cooking
  3. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Place tomatoes in a blender and puree until smooth. Pour tomato puree into pan
  5. Add vodka to the pan, simmer the sauce for 10 minutes over medium-low heat
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and parmesan cheese and simmer for another 5 minutes or until thickened. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste
  7. Pour the suace over the pasta and toss to coat. Sprinkle with parsley, parmesan cheese, and red pepper flakes, then serve

Oven baked chicken

Juicy oven baked chicken breast

A very simple and easy recipe to make for those who are on a time crunch! Oven baked chicken always comes out juicy and is a great meal filled with protein.

Ingredients needed

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Old bay seasoning
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450°F
  2. Brine the chicken (place in lukewarm water with salt to let it soak up)
  3. Brush your chicken with olive oil
  4. Rub your chicken with Old bay seasoning + any other seasoning of your choice
  5. Once the oven is pre-heated, throw your chicken in there and let sit for 15-20 minutes
  6. Remove and make sure the temperature of the chicken is atleast 165 ºF. Let it sit for 5 minutes
